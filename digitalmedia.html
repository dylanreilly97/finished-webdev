This section includes a some of my work from various design modules. I've always had a huge love for art and drawing. Ever since I was a child I've been making art. It still plays a huge part in my life and is one of my favourite pastimes. Art was my favourite subject throughout primary and secondary school. My love for art is definitely one of the reasons I chose to do a creative digital media course in college.

I mainly focus on landscape and realism works, however, I still draw lot using cartooning effects. Ialso use Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop to create new and original artworks. I takea lot of inspiration from the artist Pablo Picasso who focused a lot on cubism.
